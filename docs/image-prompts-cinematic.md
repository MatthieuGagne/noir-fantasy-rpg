# Image Prompts — Cinematic Style

## Group 1 — The Promenade, The Heights, 3am
*Reid, Iris, Margot, Gideon, Vesper*

> Dark cinematic digital painting. Photorealistic painterly rendering — no outlines, no cel-shading. Rich fabric and texture detail visible in clothing (leather grain, coat weave, layered materials). Dramatic volumetric backlighting with rim light separating characters from a dark atmospheric background. Rain and mist creating depth haze. Mostly desaturated dark palette with selective neon color pops (cyan, violet, amber). Wet reflective ground with character reflections. High contrast between foreground figures and background. Characters grouped close, facing forward, lit like a movie poster. Style: dark fantasy concept art, WLOP, Guweiz, cinematic CRPG cover illustration.
>
> Five characters standing in a line on a rain-soaked corporate boulevard at 3am. The district is immaculate and subtly wrong — too clean, no grime. Along the walls, glowing blue-white kiosks pulse like civic infrastructure. Megacorp signage reflects broken in the wet stone. Dark sky, heavy rain.
>
> Left to right: (1) A lean pale Irish-Scottish man, mid-40s, dark brown hair going grey at the temples, dark grey eyes. Long worn olive coat, the ghost of a scratched-off insignia on the breast. Amber and tobacco brown palette. (2) A compact Southeast Asian woman, looks younger than she is. Short asymmetric hair, bright vivid electric red — dramatically cut, jaw-length on one side, nearly shaved on the other. Dark eyes with a striking iridescent quality, clearly artificial — like oil on dark water, shifting color in the neon. A matte deep teal catsuit, high collar, hood worn down, hidden pockets as faint panel seam lines, copper accent at seam piping and collar edge. Flat-soled integrated boots, one continuous silhouette. Teal and copper palette. (3) A tall Northern European woman, long straight blonde hair loose on both sides of her face, pale grey eyes. Deep burgundy high-collared structured coat; a small device at her collarbone glows faintly cold. Burgundy and wine palette. (4) A massive dark-skinned man, close-cropped fade, short maintained beard. Field-worn slate-blue longcoat, gold badge on chest, a neural interface implant uncovered at the temple. Slate blue palette. (5) A mixed-race Black woman, medium brown skin, platinum white natural-textured hair. Near-black coat with a deep violet undertone that only appears where neon catches it. She stands slightly apart, angled a degree off from the others. Deep violet and near-black palette.

## Group 2 — The Meridian, Night
*Soraya, Āio, Kos, Paz, Doc Karim*

> Dark cinematic digital painting. Photorealistic painterly rendering — no outlines, no cel-shading. Rich fabric and texture detail visible in clothing (leather grain, coat weave, layered materials). Dramatic volumetric backlighting with rim light separating characters from a dark atmospheric background. Rain and mist creating depth haze. Mostly desaturated dark palette with selective neon color pops (cyan, violet, amber). Wet reflective ground with character reflections. High contrast between foreground figures and background. Characters grouped close, facing forward, lit like a movie poster. Style: dark fantasy concept art, WLOP, Guweiz, cinematic CRPG cover illustration.
>
> Five characters standing in a line in a district where the geometry is visibly wrong — a staircase ends flush against a ceiling in the background, a doorframe leans at an impossible angle, standing water reflects light sources that don't exist above it. Fraying government cordon tape at a collapsed wall. Sick magentas and cold greens in the neon signage. Rain falling at inconsistent angles.
>
> Left to right: (1) An Iranian/Persian woman, mid-to-late 50s, warm olive skin, sharp precise features, long loose electric blue hair. Long fitted white tech coat — the only white figure in the scene. Glowing blue interface ports at wrist and temple. White and cold blue palette. (2) A tall, powerfully muscular Māori / Pacific Islander figure — non-binary, androgynous, the physique of a serious fighter built through decades of practice: broad shoulders, defined arms, but with feminine softness in the face and bearing. Inspiration: Rhea Ripley's build and presence — muscular yet unmistakably feminine. Traditional tā moko tattoo covering the cheeks and curving up toward the forehead, worn openly. Natural black hair in a warrior topknot. Dark sleeveless outer layers, bare arms, no visible technology or implants anywhere on the body. Earth brown and bone white palette. Arms crossed, completely at rest. (3) A severely pale Russian man, late 20s, sharp jaw and prominent brow, deep violet/indigo hair worn loose. Long dark high-collared coat, permanent dark circles, a scar at his collarbone. Eyes focused somewhere slightly to the left of everyone else. Cold black and deep grey palette. (4) A Latina woman, warm brown skin, long bright orange braids, dark expressive eyes. Battered dark leather jacket with vivid red lining visible where it opens. A heavily modified electric guitar half-raised from its back strap. Burnt orange and deep red palette. (5) A stocky South Asian man, mid-50s, warm medium-dark skin, dark hair gone fully grey and slightly unkempt. Heavily pocketed patched dark green utility jacket, medical supplies — vials, syringes — visible on the exterior straps. A jury-rigged medical monocle over one eye, improvised cyberware with a faint diagnostic glow, the kind of equipment built by someone who has been practicing without a license for decades. Deep worn green palette.
